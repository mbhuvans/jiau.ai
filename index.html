Instructional Strategy EngineDecides learning or support approach

Adaptive Personalisation EngineDecides next topic, difficulty, or support path

Pedagogical Scaffolding Enginehint → guided → partial → full

Intervention & Recommendation EngineDecides when to escalate or recommend
Monitors for persistent errors, disengagement & performance drops.
→ Output: Teacher/parent notification or escalation alert
Key differentiator: the model is NOT the decision-maker. This layer determines what and how to teach before any AI generation occurs.
